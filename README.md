## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, product performance, and business insights. Python and various data analysis libraries were used to clean, analyze, and visualize the data.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Perform initial data inspection.
- Analyze customer demographics.
- Identify monthly and quarterly sales trends.
- Analyze product category performance.
- Visualize relationships between numerical variables.
- Generate actionable business recommendations based on data.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

---

## 📂 Dataset

- **Dataset:** Retail Sales Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Inspection
- Dataset Shape
- Column Information
- Data Types
- Missing Value Analysis

### 2. Descriptive Statistics
- Mean
- Median
- Mode
- Standard Deviation

### 3. Time Series Analysis
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Quarterly Sales Trend

### 4. Customer Demographics
- Age Distribution
- Gender Distribution

### 5. Product Analysis
- Revenue by Product Category
- Number of Sales by Product Category

> **Note:** The selected dataset contains product categories instead of individual product names. Therefore, product category analysis has been performed instead of top-selling product analysis.

### 6. Correlation Analysis
- Correlation Heatmap

### 7. Additional Visualization
- Box Plot for Total Purchase Amount

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Monthly sales fluctuate throughout the year.
- Quarterly sales reveal changes in customer demand.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across different age groups.
- Product categories contribute differently to overall revenue.
- Correlation analysis helps identify relationships between numerical variables.
- Some transactions have significantly higher purchase amounts than others.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase promotional campaigns during low-sales months.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.
- Design targeted campaigns based on customer demographics.
- Reward loyal and high-value customers through membership programs.
- Use sales trends for inventory planning and demand forecasting.
