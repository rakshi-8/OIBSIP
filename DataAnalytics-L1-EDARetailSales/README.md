# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data

## AICTE Oasis Infobyte Internship – Data Analytics

### Task 1: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to identify customer purchasing patterns, sales trends, and business insights using Python.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Dataset Name:** Retail Sales Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Number of Records:** 1000
- **Number of Features:** 9

### Dataset Columns

- Transaction ID
- Date
- Customer ID
- Gender
- Age
- Product Category
- Quantity
- Price per Unit
- Total Amount

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

- Loaded and inspected the dataset
- Checked dataset shape, columns, and data types
- Verified missing values
- Generated descriptive statistics
- Calculated mean, median, mode, and standard deviation
- Performed monthly and quarterly sales trend analysis
- Analyzed customer demographics (Age and Gender)
- Analyzed product categories
- Created a correlation heatmap
- Created additional visualizations
- Documented observations using Markdown cells
- Provided business recommendations based on the findings

---

## 📈 Visualizations

The project includes:

- Monthly Sales Trend
- Quarterly Sales Trend
- Gender Distribution
- Age Distribution
- Product Category Analysis
- Revenue by Product Category
- Correlation Heatmap
- Additional Business Insight Visualization

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- The dataset contains 1000 retail transactions with no missing values.
- Clothing is the most frequently purchased product category.
- Customer ages are widely distributed, with an average age of approximately 41 years.
- Sales patterns vary across different months and quarters.
- Product category performance can help businesses improve inventory planning and marketing strategies.

---

## 📋 Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory for high-performing product categories such as Clothing.
2. Launch targeted marketing campaigns based on customer demographics.
3. Monitor seasonal sales trends to improve stock management and promotional planning.
